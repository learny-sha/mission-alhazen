Ramadhan Prayers

Dua for Sahur and Iftar - First Mission

Sahur Prayers

Prayers for the pre-dawn meal

Intention for Sahur

نَوَيْتُ صَوْمَ غَدٍ عَنْ أَدَاءِ فَرْضِ شَهْرِ رَمَضَانَ هَذِهِ السَّنَةِ لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى

Nawaitu shauma ghadin 'an ada'i fardhi shahri Ramadhaana haadzihis sanati lillaahi ta'aala

I intend to fast tomorrow to fulfill the obligation of Ramadan this year for the sake of Allah Ta'ala

Prayer Before Eating Sahur

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيمِ

Bismillaahir Rahmaanir Rahiim

In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful

Dua After Sahur

اَللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ مِنْ فَضْلِكَ

Allahumma inni as'aluka min fadhlika

O Allah, I ask You for Your bounty

Morning Remembrance

أَصْبَحْنَا وَأَصْبَحَ الْمُلْكُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ

Ashbahna wa ashbahal mulku lillahi rabbil 'aalamiin

We have entered the morning and the dominion belongs to Allah, Lord of the worlds

Iftar Prayers

Prayers for breaking the fast

Dua When Breaking Fast

اللّهُمَّ لَكَ صُمْتُ وَبِكَ آمَنْتُ وَعَلَى رِزْقِكَ أَفْطَرْت بِرَحْمَتِكَ يَا اَرْحَمَ الرَّحِمِيْنَ

Allahummalakasumtu wabika aamantu wa’alarizqika afthortu birohmatikaya ar-hamarrahimin.

Ya Allah, the Most Generous of all, for You I fast, and with Your provision and mercy I break my fast.

Short Iftar Dua

ذَهَبَ الظَّمَأُ وَابْتَلَّتِ الْعُرُوقُ وَثَبَتَ الْأَجْرُ إِنْ شَاءَ اللَّهُ

Dzahabadh-dhama'u wabtallatil 'uruuqu wa tsabatal ajru insya Allaah

The thirst is gone, the veins are moistened, and the reward is confirmed, if Allah wills

Gratitude Prayer

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَعَانَنِي فَصُمْتُهُ وَرَزَقَنِي فَأَفْطَرْتُهُ

Alhamdulillahil ladzi a'aanani fa shumtuhu wa razaqani fa afthartuhu

Praise be to Allah who has helped me to fast and has provided sustenance for me to break my fast

Before Eating Iftar

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيمِ، اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لَنَا فِيمَا رَزَقْتَنَا وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ

Bismillahir Rahmanir Rahim, Allahumma barik lana fima razaqtana wa qina 'adzaaban-naar

In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful. O Allah, bless us in what You have provided us and protect us from the punishment of the Fire

Evening Gratitude

أَمْسَيْنَا وَأَمْسَى الْمُلْكُ لِلَّهِ، وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ

Amsaina wa amsal mulku lillahi, walhamdulillah

We have entered the evening and the dominion belongs to Allah, and all praise is due to Allah

After Eating Iftar

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَطْعَمَنَا وَسَقَانَا وَجَعَلَنَا مُسْلِمِينَ

Alhamdulillahil ladzi ath'amana wa saqana wa ja'alana muslimiin

Praise be to Allah who has fed us, given us drink, and made us Muslims
